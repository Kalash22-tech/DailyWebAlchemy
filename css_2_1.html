Grilled Cheese Sandwich with Bacon and Tomato

Ingredients

  • 4 slices of crispy bacon
  • 2 fresh tomato slices
  • 2 slices of melty cheddar cheese
  • 2 slices of your favorite bread
  • 2 tablespoons of butter

Instructions

1. Cook the bacon in a pan until crispy, then set aside.
2. Spread butter on one side of each bread slice.
3. Place a slice of bread, buttered side down, in a pan over medium heat.
4. Layer cheese, bacon, and tomato on top, then cover with another slice of cheese and the second slice of bread, buttered side up.
5. Cook until golden brown, then flip and repeat until the cheese melts.
6. Let it cool slightly, slice, and enjoy!
